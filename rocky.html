Meet my cat Rocky

Rocky

This is my cat Rocky. He lived with me in an apartment last year, where I took lots of pictures with him. Now he lives with my parents, but he's coming back to Ann Arbor with me next year!

Rocky is my beloved pet cat who is probably around eight or nine years old now and has been my companion for most of them. He's a red tabby and white Exotic shorthair and used to be a show cat; he almost became a grand champion in just one cat show (he earned 199 of the 200 points needed to become a grand champion in the first show) and was on his way to becoming a regional winner in the Great Lakes region of the Cat Fanciers Association, but my mom's health issues cut Rocky's cat show circuit short.
